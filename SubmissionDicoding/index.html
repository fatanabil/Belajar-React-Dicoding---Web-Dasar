Culinary

Indonesian cuisine is one of the culinary traditions with very rich and flavorful. Here are some popular cuisine that you should try if you have chance to go to Indonesia.

Rendang - Padang

Rendang, an authentic Indonesian dish from Padang, is often praised as one of the world's best foods. Its tender beef is cooked to perfection with flavorful herbs and spices. Because of its juicy texture and a perfect balance of spices, rendang is a favorite among the tourists!

Gudeg - Yogyakarta

Gudeg is an iconic sweet dish of Yogyakarta. It is made from unripe jackfruit or gori combined with thick coconut milk and palm sugar. To get the authentic flavor of gudeg, people often cook it in an earthen pot over a wood or charcoal fire. This delicious treat is often served with condiments, such as chicken, hard-boiled eggs, tempeh, tofu, and sambal krecek or crispy beef skin with chili sauce. It is sold commonly on the streets of Yogyakarta, but the most famous places to buy gudeg are Wijilan and Barek.

Rawon - Surabaya

Rawon, a delicious beef stew, is a famous dish of Surabaya. Traditional herbs like lemongrass, chili, ginger, lime leaves, and kluwek or black nuts are added to the slow-braised, tender meat. Kluwek is what gives a rich flavor and a unique black color to the stew. It is said that rawon was primarily a plebeian dish in the past, but its scrumptious favor soon became popular among the Indonesian royalty!

Bakso - Malang

Served with bakso and fried wontons in a delicious warm broth, bakwan Malang is an Indonesian dish that's perfect for the rainy season. Originating from Malang, this dish has been adapted from an Indonesian-Chinese tradition and is made from beef or chicken meatballs. This hearty comfort food dish sometimes includes vermicelli rice noodles, green onions, and fried shallots. Sweet soy sauce and sambal are also served to customers who want to add more flavor to their meal. A famous restaurant that serves the best bakso Malang is Bakso President Malang.

Satay Lilit - Bali

Bali is not only known for its exotic beaches but also for its mouth-watering delicacies. One dish that is sold by street vendors in Bali is satay lilit, an Indonesian street food dish made from minced meat. This dish can be created using different types of meats, but pork is the most popular form used. Herbs and spices like lemongrass, galangal, chili, and lime leaves are added to give it a fresh spicy taste. This Indonesian dish is a must-try when you get the chance to visit Bali!

Tourism

Indonesia has a lot of stunning tourism. Every year lots of foreigners spend their holiday visiting some tourism in Indonesia. These are some popular place / tourism in Indonesia that you might want to visit.

Bali

Bali is very popular among travelers from abroad or domestic since a long time ago. Bali is very famous for its amazing places like Kuta Beach, Uluwatu Temple, or Tanah Lot. Besides that, Bali is rich in culture which is also an attraction for tourists. And there are many more hidden gems in Bali.

Raja Ampat

People who love the sea will know this place. This is Raja Ampat located in West Papua. Raja Ampat means 'The Four Kings'. Raja Ampat is very famous for its underwater natural beauty which is often used as a spot for diving. Raja Ampat is home to a wide variety of species of fish, coral, and other marine animals.

Jakarta

Jakarta is known as the capital city of Indonesia with a population of 10 million people of various ethnicities and backgrounds from all over Indonesia. Not only about skyscrapers, but the City of Jakarta also has a history that is quite interesting to review. There are several historical sites such as 'Kota Tua', National Museum and National Monument. There are several theme park such as Dunia Fantasi, Ancol, and many others.

Yogyakarta

Known as the City of Students, Yogyakarta also has many tourist attractions and also a very rich culture, especially Javanese culture. There is Prambanan Temple which is one of the most beautiful temples in the world. In addition, there is a historical tour, namely the Keraton which is very thick with Javanese culture. And don't forget, Malioboro Road is a place for us to shop for souvenirs of Yogyakarta.

Komodo National Park

Komodo National Park can be one of your tourist destinations. There you can see the Komodo dragon which is the main attraction of this tour. UNESCO has also declared that Komodo National Park is a world heritage site. Besides seeing Komodo dragons, you can also enjoy the beauty of the islands in East Nusa Tenggara.
